# Design 2 Concept

Visual direction:
- Contemporary editorial archive
- Almost-black background
- Warm ivory dossier panel
- Large serif headline
- Technical mono metadata
- Thin rules, asymmetric spacing, dossier-like project rows

Core copy:
- `TheArquive`
- `A living archive of projects that do not fit inside one discipline.`
- `TheArquive collects books, tools, systems, prototypes, research fragments and digital experiments — not as a showcase of finished objects, but as a map of work in progress.`
- `Not a portfolio. An archive of direction.`
- `Featured Projects`
- `A selection of current and archived work — organised not by polish, but by intent.`

Structural requirements:
- Hero + editorial manifesto card
- Project index in dossier/list format
- Category and status filters
- Archive note about each new folder becoming a new entry
- Sober link language: `Open dossier`, `Read notes`, `Source / Repo`, `Live build`
